Who am I

As a current student at CoderAcademy Melbourne, I'm on a mission to kickstart my career as a developer and join the leaders, game-changers and rule-breakers redefining the tech scene.
After spending the better part of 10 years as a public servant in Brisbane, I decided it was time to find my 'why' - a way of turning my passions for innovation, UX design, coding and tech into a career. Now, it's only a matter of 'when'.
For me, there's nothing more satisfying than seeing a project come to life, and better still, being part of the team that's made it happen. I love a challenge, and see every hurdle as a learning opportunity that ultimately strengthens the end product. So whether you're hiring, looking to collaborate, or just want to connect, I'd love to hear from you.
